The Atelier

Not a salon.
An atelier.

Most salons sell appointments. Le Salon sells outcomes — the turn of a layer at the cheekbone, the exact warmth of a ribbon of colour, the quiet confidence of hands that have done this ten thousand times.

In the heart of Kathmandu, our chairs are workbenches and our mirrors are the only critics we answer to. We take fewer clients and give them more time — because craft doesn’t rush, and neither should you.

— The Le Salon team
